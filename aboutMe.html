About Miguel Brown

Data Analyst and BI analyst skilled in SQL, Tableau, Power BI, Excel and Python

I'm a recent college graduate with a BSc in Computer Science, and I'm incredibly passionate about pursuing a career in data analysis and business intelligence (BI). My educational background has equipped me with a solid foundation in computer science principles and programming, and I have been actively honing my skills in SQL, Excel, Tableau, and Python to excel in the field of data analysis.
During my time in college, I gained valuable experience working on various projects that involved data manipulation, analysis, and visualization. These experiences allowed me to develop a keen eye for patterns and trends within datasets, and I thoroughly enjoyed uncovering meaningful insights to drive informed decision-making.
Currently, I am enthusiastically immersing myself in the world of data analysis and BI by further expanding my knowledge and practical expertise. I am continuously learning and practicing SQL to efficiently query databases, Excel for data organization and analysis, Tableau for visually presenting data, and Python for advanced data manipulation and modeling.
My goal is to leverage my strong analytical skills and technical expertise to help organizations make data-driven decisions and solve complex problems. I am excited about the endless possibilities that data analysis and BI offer in optimizing processes, identifying business opportunities, and driving growth.
